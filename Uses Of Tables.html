Parts Of A Computer

Parts Of Computer Name Of The Parts Uses of The Parts
Monitor
 A computer monitor is an output device that displays information in pictorial form.
Mouse
 The mouse is a hand-held device that transmits your commands to the computer by controlling the movement of the cursor/pointer on the computer screen.
C.P.U
 C.P.U/Central Processing Unit calculates and interprets instructions while you're surfing the web, creating documents, playing games, or running software programs.
U.P.S
 An uninterruptible power supply (UPS) is used to protect critical loads from utility-supplied power problems, including spikes, brownouts, fluctuations and power outages, all using a dedicated battery.
